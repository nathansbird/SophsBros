My Vision Statement

I was created to be loved by God and to create beautiful things that bring Him glory. To both recognize and create beautiful things. I was created to use all of my gifts, passions, and resources to glorify God and to help others to recognize why they were created and empower them to use their gifts, passions, and resources to also glorify God by loving them, giving them opportunities, and loosening the chains of injustice and oppression, both spiritually and situationally.
